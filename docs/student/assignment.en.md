# Assignment

## Step 1. Getting Started

- Accept the terms and authenticate before starting the assignment.

![Getting Started](./images/assignment1.webp)

## Step 2. Submitting Assignment

- Submit your assignment within the deadline.

![Submitting Assignment](./images/assignment2.webp)

- Grading is in progress during the grading period.

![Submitting Assignment](./images/assignment3.webp)

!!! failure "Pre-submission Plagiarism Check"

    Plagiarism check is performed when submitting assignments.
    If it meets the plagiarism criteria set for the assignment, you cannot submit.

    ![Submitting Assignment](./images/assignment4.webp)

## Step 3. Grading Review

- Grading is completed with rubrics. Once grading is complete, grades are published and the grade appeal period begins.

![Grading Review](./images/assignment5.webp)

- Submit an appeal within the grade appeal period.

![Grading Review](./images/assignment6.webp)

## Step 4. Final Grade

- Re-grade if necessary based on the appeal and finalize the score.

![Final Grade](./images/assignment7.webp)
